About McClure

Welcome to McClure Electric, Inc.

McClure Electric, Inc. founded in July of 1966 by Thomas D. McClure as a full service Electrical Contractor. We provide complete electrical system upgrades and maintenance needs for the San Francisco Bay Area.

McClure Electric, Inc. is experienced in Commercial, Industrial, Hospitality, Healthcare and Residential projects.

McClure Communications was established in 2001 as a result of the data and cabling needs of our customers. This division specializes in advanced structured cabling projects for voice/data networks.
